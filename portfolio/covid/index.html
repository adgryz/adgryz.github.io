Helping people to work in better conditions

The product is a complex mobile app that will facilitate short-term rental of a place for remote work. The app will enable searching and booking places in a close location adapted to remote work.

The users of Micro

We have identified two users groups of our app.The first group are people working remotely, whom we want to ensure with access to the database of premises that meet their needs; we called them guests. The second group are people who are interested in providing remote work space for a fee; we called them hosts

The group of respondents

The respondents were recruited by the snowball sampling technique based on the screener. The group consisted of 15 millennials from cities with more than 100 thousand inhabitants.

Personas

After the interviews with the respondents we created three personas, which helped us understand users' needs, experiences, behaviours and goals. We paid attention to the advantages of working remotely, such as flexible working hours, saving time for commuting, the possibility of combining work with home duties. However, we also noticed disadvantages such as lower employee productivity, dispersion, lack of direct contact with colleagues, a work- life balance problem, technical problems related to bad work equipment or low internet connection On the other hand, the remote work situation brings problems for a second group of the users of our product. They are landlords who are struggling with a less interest of a tenancy. As a result, many flats are empty, as students and employees give up their rent and returned to their family homes.
