Marvel - amazing but complex

Me and my husband, who is a frontend developer, are huge fans of Marvel Cinematic Universe, which is one of the biggest cinematic universes if not the biggest one. To this day there are 24 movies and 4 series and there is coming more in the near future. So we decided to create together a website for newcomers and everyone else who wants to complement their knowledge.

One page business plan - Lean Canvas

We started our work with paper and pens. Our first steps was completing Lean Canvas template. This is a great way to start, because you can develop big picture of whole project and decide if the project is promising on an early stage.

The Screener
